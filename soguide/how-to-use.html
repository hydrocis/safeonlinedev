How to use this site.

Why a digital security guide?

Privacy and digital security threats are credible threats which can be experienced by users of the internet and digital technology tools. In Africa and Nigeria in particular, the threats are more worrisome as there are no well-defined laws and policies that cater to violations of privacy and digital security of citizens.

With growing internet penetration, increase in the use of smart phones, mobile & web applications and the number of people accessing these tools; so is the increase to their vulnerabilities to attacks and loss of privacy.
